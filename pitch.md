# Nagarik Sahayak — 3-Minute Stage Pitch Script

---

## [0:00–0:30] The Problem

> "India has over 700 central and state government schemes — PM-KISAN, Vidyasiri, Ayushman Bharat, Sukanya Samriddhi. Crores of rupees allocated every year.
>
> But here's the tragedy: **the people who need these schemes the most — a farmer in Mandya, a daily-wage worker in Varanasi — they don't know they're eligible.** They can't read 40-page PDF guidelines. They don't have English literacy. They don't know which portal to visit.
>
> The gap isn't policy. **The gap is access.**"

---

## [0:30–1:15] The Demo

> "Let me show you Nagarik Sahayak — the citizen's AI assistant that bridges this gap."

**[Open the app on phone/screen]**

> "It's a WhatsApp-style chat. No downloads, no logins — just a phone number and an OTP."

**[Type: `Mera beta 10th pass hai`]**

> "A father in Karnataka types: *Mera beta 10th pass hai* — my son passed 10th class."

**[Point at the streaming bullets]**

> "Watch what happens. The system is scanning actual scheme PDFs… checking eligibility criteria… generating a filled form — all in under 2 seconds."

**[Point at the result]**

> "**Vidyasiri Scholarship — Eligible.** ₹12,000–₹24,000 per year for his son's post-10th education. Plus **PM-KISAN** — ₹6,000 per year.
>
> A pre-filled PDF is ready to download. This father can walk into the local office tomorrow with this document."

**[Tap the Hindi voice button]**

> "And for those who can't read — the entire response is read aloud in Hindi. On any device, even offline."

---

## [1:15–2:00] How It Works

> "Under the hood, this is an **MCP-powered multi-tool AI agent:**
>
> 1. **Profiler Agent** — collects user info one question at a time, in Hindi.
> 2. **Document Scanner** — MCP tool that reads scheme PDFs and extracts eligibility criteria.
> 3. **Eligibility Matcher** — compares profile against criteria. Returns per-scheme JSON: `{eligible: true, reason, benefit}`.
> 4. **PDF Generator** — auto-creates a filled eligibility report, ready for submission.
>
> Every tool call is tracked via **Agnost AI** for observability. We know which tools fire, latency, success rate — all in a live dashboard.
>
> Speech-to-text is powered by **Sarvam Saaras v3** — India's best bilingual STT model. Hindi in, English out, both ways."

---

## [2:00–2:30] The Impact

> "Let me put this in perspective:
>
> - **40 crore Indians** are eligible for schemes they've never heard of.
> - The government spends **₹8,000+ crores** on just *awareness campaigns* every year.
> - A single Nagarik Sahayak deployment to a district of 10 lakh people could redirect **₹50–100 crores** to citizens who actually qualify.
>
> This isn't a chatbot. **This is a digital rights enabler.**"

---

## [2:30–3:00] The Ask

> "We're building this as an open platform.
>
> - **Phase 1** (today): Voice + chat eligibility checker. Works in Hindi and English.
> - **Phase 2**: Real PDF ingestion — upload any government scheme PDF, the system indexes it automatically.
> - **Phase 3**: Integration with DigiLocker and state portals for one-click application submission.
>
> We're looking for **pilot partners** — state governments, NGOs, CSR programs — who want to put this in the hands of citizens.
>
> Because every Indian deserves to know: *'Aapko yeh milna chahiye.'* — **You deserve this.**
>
> Thank you."
